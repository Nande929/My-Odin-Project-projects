Chocolate Pudding

Chocolate Pudding is a rich, velvety dessert made from silky cocoa or melted chocolate blended into a creamy, spoon-soft mixture. It has a deep, comforting chocolate flavor with a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Served chilled, it’s indulgent, comforting, and perfect for anyone craving a sweet, chocolatey treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into chunks
  • 1 banana, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 cup unsweetened soy milk
  • ¼ cup raw cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons agave nectar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ cup shredded unsweetened coconut (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Place avocado, banana, soy milk, cocoa powder, agave nectar, lemon juice, and coconut into a blender.
  2. Cover, and puree until smooth.
  3. Divide into small containers.
  4. Store in the refrigerator 1 hour until set.
